Glass-to-metal seals are critical components used in various industries to create hermetic sealing between glass and metal components. These seals are widely employed in aerospace, automotive, electronics, and defense applications to protect sensitive components from environmental factors such as moisture, pressure, and temperature fluctuations. The two main types of glass-to-metal seals are matched seals and compression seals, each serving distinct purposes based on material compatibility and application requirements.
